## Discovery Notes

PSI Automation is a niche B2B industrial manufacturer of vane air motors in Sandpoint, Idaho. They have a uniquely strong technical niche: custom pneumatic air motors used in aerospace, automotive, chemical, mining, petroleum, and general manufacturing. They have a rare "Size Your Own Motor" calculator tool and NASA-cited technical documentation on their safety silencers. The site is extremely dated — looks untouched since ~2002. However, the business appears legitimate and long-standing (20+ years). Their competitor differentiation is custom engineering — not an off-the-shelf commodity. High-ticket B2B clients (engineering departments) need to trust the vendor before purchasing.

**Why flag this:** The site is so bad it actively undermines sales. Engineers evaluating vendors expect professional, credible websites. This is a high-upside opportunity — a modern site could dramatically increase conversion on inbound traffic.
