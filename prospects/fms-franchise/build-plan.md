## Competitive Positioning

FMS's new site will distinguish itself from generic franchise broker sites and outdated consulting firm websites by leading with authority proof at every scroll position: every section will reference the 900+ brands, 67-person team, or real case study outcomes. Unlike competitors who hide case studies or bury their process, FMS will foreground the A-Z process (with a visual timeline/roadmap) as proof of structured delivery. The site will also be the only franchise consulting site in the market with a proper video hub strategy — surfacing Chris Conner's educational videos as trust-builders before the first CTA. Mobile-first, fast-loading (Next.js static), and designed for business owners who are evaluating multiple firms simultaneously.
