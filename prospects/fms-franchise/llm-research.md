### Messaging Framework (Finalized from Cross-Model Synthesis)

**Primary headline theme:** *"Your business model works. Now let's build the system that lets others prove it."* — addresses validation desire + outcome framing.

**Above-fold fear to address first:** "What if I lose control of my brand?" — Perplexity, Claude, and ChatGPT all flagged this as the #1 emotional barrier.

**3 objections to preemptively dismantle (in order):**
1. "The cost is too high" → Counter: ROI framing, performance-based model, "pays for itself with first few sales"
2. "My business is too unique" → Counter: diverse case study library, unconventional brands we've franchised
3. "I'm not big enough yet" → Counter: "Some of the most explosive franchise growth stories started with a single thriving location"

**Tone:** Expert authority + empathy. Not salesy. Claude put it perfectly: "The site should function like a trusted advisor walking a business owner through their decision — not a brochure."

**Headline frameworks by buyer stage:**
- Awareness: *"Is Your Business Ready to Franchise? Find Out in 5 Minutes"*
- Consideration: *"From One Location to a National Brand: The Franchise Development Roadmap That Actually Works"*
- Decision: *"They Said My Business Was Too Unique to Franchise. Now I Have 30 Locations"*
