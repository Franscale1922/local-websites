## Prompt

You are an expert web designer and local business marketing strategist. I am building a website for a local **general and cosmetic dental practice** called **Summit Dental Group** in **Kalispell, Montana** (Flathead Valley area). The owner is **Dr. Mark Vanzant, DMD**, a decorated U.S. Air Force veteran who served 3 deployments and continues service in the Montana Army National Guard.

Please analyze and give me:

1. **Top 5 conversion-driving sections** on the best dental practice websites in 2024–2025. What sections are proven to convert hesitant patients into booked appointments? Describe each section's content, visual treatment, and placement on the page.

2. **Trust signals specific to dental practices** — what do high-performing dental sites use to overcome patient fear and hesitation? (Certifications, photos, testimonials, emergency language, guarantees, etc.)

3. **Local SEO must-haves for a dental practice** — what page structure, schema markup, and content strategy gives a local dental practice the best chance of ranking for searches like "dentist Kalispell MT" or "dental implants Kalispell"?

4. **Design/UX patterns from award-winning dental sites** — what visual design trends work best for this industry? (Color palettes, typography, imagery, layout patterns, hero formats)

5. **Competitor weaknesses** — what do most small/mid dental practice websites do poorly that a well-built site could immediately capitalize on?

6. **Recommended page structure** — what pages should a comprehensive general+cosmetic dental practice website have? List every page with a brief description of what it should contain.

7. **Montana/outdoor community considerations** — any regional or lifestyle-based messaging considerations for a practice in the Flathead Valley area of Montana?

8. **Military angle opportunity** — the owner is a veteran who accepts Tricare/ADDP insurance. How should this be presented on the website to attract military families and veteran patients?

Be specific, evidence-based where possible, and prioritize what will have the highest real-world impact on patient acquisition.
