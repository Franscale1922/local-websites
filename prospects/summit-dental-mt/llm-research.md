### TRICARE "ACN Concierge" Strategy (Gemini's Unique Insight)
Gemini provided the most detailed TRICARE operational guidance:

- **Remote eligibility:** Guard/Reserve members living >50 miles from a military Dental Treatment Facility (MTF) can self-refer to civilian care
- **DTF-referred:** When MTF cannot provide the care needed
- **ACN (Appointment Control Number):** Required from United Concordia BEFORE receiving civilian care
- **Routine care:** Covers procedures under $750/appointment (up to $1,500/year) without complex prior authorizations

**"ACN Concierge" page structure:**
- Headline: *"We know military insurance can be complicated. We are proud to handle it for you."*
- Explain DTF-referral process and 50-mile remote rule in plain language
- State explicitly: *"Our front office team will assist you in generating, submitting, or verifying your ACN"*
- TDP (Tricare Dental Program) for dependents/non-activated Guard: clearly distinguished from ADDP
- This administrative burden removal = **massive conversion driver** from military patients
